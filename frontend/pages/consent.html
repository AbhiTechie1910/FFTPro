1. Purpose of the Assessment

I understand that functional fitness assessments conducted using FFTPro are intended to evaluate physical performance parameters such as strength, balance, flexibility, endurance, mobility, and functional independence. These assessments may assist the therapist in clinical decision-making, progress tracking, and documentation.

2. Data Collected

a. Demographic Information

  • Full name, age, gender, nationality
  • System-generated Patient ID

b. Health & Assessment Data

  • Test results and performance metrics
  • Clinician observations
  • Derived measurements (angles, distances, timing)

c. Visual & Motion Data

  • Live video feed during assessment
  • Still images/extracted frames (if required for analysis)
  • Pose-estimation / biomechanical markers derived from video
Important: Video/image data is processed strictly for clinical assessment, measurement, and documentation. It is not used for advertising or unrelated commercial purposes.

3. Use of Video, Image, and Motion Data

I expressly consent to the capture, processing, and analysis of my video and image data solely for functional assessment, clinical interpretation, performance measurement, and reporting within FFTPro.

  • Raw video is not shared with third parties without explicit authorization or legal obligation.
  • Pose-estimation outputs are used to compute movement metrics and are restricted to clinical purposes.
  • Access is restricted to the treating therapist and authorized system components.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored in a secure digital environment with access controls and reasonable technical/organizational safeguards designed to mitigate unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

5. Confidentiality

My information will be treated as confidential healthcare information and will not be disclosed except:

  • Where required by applicable law or lawful request
  • Where explicitly authorized by me
  • Where required for medical/legal compliance within permitted limits

6. Voluntary Participation

Participation is voluntary. I may withdraw consent at any time prior to or during assessment. Withdrawal may limit the ability to continue/complete assessments and may affect record completeness.

7. Limitation of Liability

FFTPro is an assessment support tool and does not provide independent medical diagnosis. Final clinical interpretation remains with the licensed therapist. FFTPro and the therapist are not liable for outcomes resulting from incomplete, inaccurate, or interrupted capture beyond reasonable control.

8. Patient Rights

I have the right to ask questions, request clarification on data handling, request access to my records, and request deletion where legally and clinically permissible.

9. Consent Declaration

By checking the box below, I confirm I have read and understood this consent and voluntarily agree to participate in the assessments and data practices described.

“Start Assessment” remains disabled until consent is provided.

This consent applies to newly added patients prior to their first assessment. Existing patients with recorded consent may bypass this step as per system records.
