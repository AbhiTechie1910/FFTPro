Instructions

Balance

Assesses: static balance + postural control (fall risk screen).

  1. Stand barefoot or with standard footwear (as per protocol), near a wall/chair for safety.
  2. Keep arms on hips (or by sides if your protocol says), look straight ahead.
  3. Lift one foot off the ground (do not touch the standing leg).
  4. Start timing when the foot leaves the ground and posture is stable.
  5. Stop timing if:
    • Raised foot touches the floor
    • Raised foot touches the stance leg
    • Stance foot moves/steps/hops
    • Arms leave hips (if required)
  6. Perform on both sides. Record the best hold time (seconds) for each leg.

Tip: Use Record Hold at the best stable moment, and it will fill the correct side automatically.
