Instructions

Flexibility

Assesses: hamstrings + lower back flexibility (posterior chain).

  1. Place a chair against a wall to prevent sliding.
  2. Patient sits upright; straight back; one foot flat on the floor.
  3. Extend the test leg forward: heel on floor, toes up (dorsiflexed).
  4. Keep the knee straight (no bending). Reach forward with the hand.
  5. Hold the farthest position for ~2 seconds.
  6. Score (per leg):
    • +1 if fingertip goes beyond toe (no compensation)
    • 0 if fingertip just touches toe (no compensation)
    • -1 if fingertip does not reach toe
    • -1 if any compensatory movement/postural sway is detected (auto-fail + snapshot)
  7. Do 2 trials per leg; record the best score for each side.

Tip: Choose the test leg (Left/Right) under the camera controls. The system will auto-record after a stable hold, or you can press Record Score.
