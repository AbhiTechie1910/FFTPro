Instructions

Upper Body Strength

Assesses: biceps strength and upper-limb endurance.

  1. Stand upright, feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Keep upper arm close to the torso; avoid swinging the trunk.
  3. Start with arm extended (elbow open), then curl up to a flexed elbow position.
  4. Perform as many controlled reps as possible for 30 seconds.
  5. Count only full reps (extension → flexion).

Record Results

Total repetitions completed in 30 seconds.
