Instructions

Agility / Dynamic Balance
  1. Sit on a chair. On “Go”, stand up and walk as fast and safely as possible.
  2. Walk to the turning mark (8 ft), turn around, walk back, and sit down.
  3. Time is recorded from start movement to sitting back down.
  4. System flags instability: excessive trunk lean, lateral sway, arm swing, hands-on-thigh support.
  5. When instability is detected, the system captures a snapshot.

Calibration: click two on-screen labelled points (A,B,…) that represent 8 ft apart on the floor.
