Instructions

Lower Body Strength

Assesses: lower-limb strength and functional mobility.

  1. Place a chair against a wall to prevent sliding.
  2. Patient sits in the middle of the chair, back straight, feet flat, hip-width apart.
  3. Arms crossed over the chest (no pushing off thighs/armrests).
  4. On “Go”, stand up fully (hips and knees extended), then sit down until buttocks touch the seat.
  5. Repeat as many complete stand–sit cycles as possible for 30 seconds.
  6. Count only full repetitions with good form.
  7. If the system auto-records or you press Record Result, it will populate the result field.
